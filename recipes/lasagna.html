Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of tender pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The process begins with a hearty meat sauce made by sautéing onions and garlic, then browning ground beef and simmering it with tomatoes and Italian herbs.

Meanwhile, a smooth béchamel sauce is prepared by creating a roux with butter and flour, then adding milk and seasoning it with nutmeg. The lasagna is assembled by layering the meat sauce, pasta sheets, béchamel, and cheese in a baking dish, repeating the layers, and finishing with a generous cheese topping. Baked until golden and bubbly, the lasagna is then rested to set before serving, offering a comforting and flavorful dish perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

Meat sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Béchamel sauce:
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Prepare the Béchamel sauce
  4. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
  6. Bake
  7. Rest and serve
