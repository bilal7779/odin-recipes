Pizza recipe

Description:

Making a homemade pizza involves a series of straightforward steps that result in a delicious, customizable dish. Begin by preparing the dough: activate yeast in warm water with a pinch of sugar, then mix in flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead the dough until it is smooth and elastic, allowing it to rise until it doubles in size.

Once the dough is ready, roll it out to your desired thickness and spread a layer of tomato sauce over it. Add a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings, such as pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives. Bake the pizza in a hot oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil and fresh basil for an extra touch of flavor. This process creates a perfect blend of crispy crust, melted cheese, and savory toppings, making for an enjoyable homemade meal.

Ingredients

Dough
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp of active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
Toppings
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers

Steps

  1. Prepare dough
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Roll Out Dough
  5. Assemble pizza
  6. Bake
  7. Serve
